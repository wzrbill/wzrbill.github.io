About Me

I am a PhD student in computer science with interests in graph algorithms. My recent work focuses on scalable graph labeling techniques and shortest path computation. I got my Bachalor's degree in Commincation and Engineering from Shandong University. I got my Master's degree in Computer Technology from Guangzhou University, supervised by Prof. Fan Zhang and co-supervised by Prof. Dian Ouyang. I am currently pursuing my PhD degree in University of New South Wales, supervised by Prof. Wenjie Zhang and co-supervised by Prof. Dong Wen.
